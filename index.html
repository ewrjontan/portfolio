Hello, I'm Jonathan

I'm A Crime Scene Investigator

But in my spare time, I like to build things.

I've been working in a forensic discipline for almost ten years. During this time, I have also been teaching myself the ins-and-outs of web development. From the start, I found coding fascinating. Little did I know, that an itch I've been longing for had finally been scratched.

I have gone through several online curriculums, including Code Academy, Free Code Camp, The Odin Project as well as NuCamp. Each time, learning something new and helping me expand my knowledge and understanding of even the most basic of concepts.

Often losing track of time at my desk while working on personal projects of mine. What started as an excuse to keep my mind active, has become a solid passion of mine. I love learning and I am at a point where I would like to turn this little hobby of mine into a career.

I have had the pleasure of building and developing using the following languages and libraries:

HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, React, React Native, Node.js, express.js, MongoDB
